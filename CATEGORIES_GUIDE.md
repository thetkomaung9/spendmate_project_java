## Best Practices

### Consistency

- **Always use the same category** for similar transactions
- Example: Always categorize your morning coffee as "Food & Dining"

### Specificity

- **Choose the most specific category** available
- Don't default to "Other" when a better fit exists

### Monthly Review

- **Review categories monthly** to ensure accurate classification
- Adjust if you find patterns of miscategorization

### Custom Needs

- If tracking a specific expense type frequently, consider if you need a more detailed system
- Example: Pet owners might want to track pet expenses separately

---

## Category Selection Tips

### When in Doubt:

1. **Ask: "What is the primary purpose of this expense?"**
2. **Consider: "Would this make sense in my monthly budget review?"**
3. **Be consistent with previous similar expenses**

### Common Confusions:

**Groceries vs. Food & Dining:**

- Groceries = food you cook at home
- Food & Dining = prepared food eaten out

**Shopping vs. Other categories:**

- Be specific when possible (Entertainment for books, Personal Care for cosmetics)
- Use Shopping for general retail

**Utilities vs. Subscriptions:**

- Utilities = essential home services
- Subscriptions = optional entertainment/services
